How to Play

Liar's Dice: Each player rolls 5 dice privately. Take turns bidding on total count of a face value across all dice.

Bids must increase. Face 6 is wild (counts as any face).

Call Liar! to challenge the last bid. If the bid was correct, the bidder wins. Otherwise, the challenger wins.
